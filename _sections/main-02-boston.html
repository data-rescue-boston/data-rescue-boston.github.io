Data Rescue Boston

Events

  • Harvard 2/1/2017
  • MIT 2/18/2017

DataRescue Boston is a series of events and efforts organized by professors, researchers, students, librarians, and activists from MIT, Harvard, Tufts, and Northeastern. It is part of a larger national movement headed by the Enviromental Data & Governance Initiative and the UPenn Program in the Enviromental Humanities.

To get involved:

  • Attend or volunteer at one of our upcoming events
  • Sponsor an event or donate to EDGI
